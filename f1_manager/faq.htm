1: Can I continue or register with the team I managed last year?
A: No, all teams have been deleted and everyone starts with a clean sheet. F1Manager wants to offer every manager the same opportunity at the start of the season and therefore everyone starts with the same budget.
top

2: How do I sell a driver, team or engine?
A: Go to your team, click the "Sell" button behind the part you want to replace, choose a new driver, team or engine that you want to buy and click the "Buy"-button. Make sure that your budget allows the transfer.
top

3: I don't have a team yet, but I have registered and can't get to my team.
A: Untill you have officially created and registered your own team, you must login in the "Register"-section. Fill out your mailaddress and password and you can start creating your team.
top

4: I did not receive some points for a certain race?
A: A driver, team or engine will start to score points for your team from the moment he has been selected by you. Previous points scored by that driver, team or engine are not included in the transfer to your team.
top

5: How does the game work?
A: The game is based on the actual achievements of the participating teams in the World Championship Formula One. Based on the results in each Grand Prix every manager is awarded points for the performance of his drivers, team and engine.
top

6: I am not able to buy a certain driver, team or engine.
A: Make sure that you have enough budget to allow you the purchase of that particular driver, team or engine.
top

7: I can't make any changes to my team.
A: During a Grand Prix the database is closed. This means the database is closed one hour before the start of the qualifying sessions and will open again on monday morning after processing of all the race results.
top

8: How do I start a subleague/how do I join a subleague?
A: You can organise a subleague yourself by going to the Subleague-section and starting a subleague. Being the initiator you get to choose a name, login and password for the league. Then you can invite others to join the subleague by giving them the login and password of the subleague. Joining an existing subleague is only possible by using the login and password of the subleague. These will have to be obtained from the initiator or other participants in this subleague. Easiest is to register by using the "Join a subleague"-button on your teampage.
top

9: Can I change my mailaddress/password/address data myself?
A: Sorry, these kind of changes can only be made by the staff of F1Manager. A request to make such a change can be sent to ronik@iname.com

10: I have registered with more than one team. Can I delete a team myself?
A: Sorry once again, for deleting a team the same procedure applies and this means it has to be requested by sending an e-mail to ronik@iname.com
