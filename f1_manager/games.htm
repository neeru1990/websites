F1 World Grand Prix 99 DEMO

F1 World Grand Prix 99 is the follow up to the Official Formula 1 Racing game. This new game in the franchise features all the teams, drivers, and circuits from the 1999 Formula 1 season. A whole host of graphical and gameplay improvements have been added, and the speed, intuitiveness, and fun offered by the first game in the series has been retained (see screen shots).

Lankhor's brand-new game engine has been developed to enable the player to experience a new level of simulation. No detail has been spared to give the best representation of a Formula 1 race car. All the main settings can be altered in the pits, but the player will never become confused by a multitude of options.

Take part in the whole weekend or simply take part on race day. The game enables up to 12 players to race against each other (PC version only). There is also the ability to replay the race from any camera and any car. All replays can be saved and given to other players to repeat or improve. All the usual camera angles are included, plus new intelligent cameras that include camera angles true to the real Grand Prix circuit placements.
