For each point scored your account will be raised with 0.1m Euro.



When selling a driver, team or engine you'll get half the value back. This money must be used to buy a new driver, team or engine. For example; if you sell Mika Häkkinen at a value of 22.0 M Euro, you'll get half of that money refunded and can use this to buy a new Top driver. Untill the first Grand Prix starts on March 12 you can transfer without losing money.
