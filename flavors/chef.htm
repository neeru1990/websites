Dear Guest,

I come from a family of food connoisseurs and enthusiasts and have been very lucky to have grown up in Mumbai (Bombay), India. Mumbai is a modern day melting pot of various ethnic communities, indigenous to India as well as from abroad. There you shall find some of the worlds finest restaurants as well as the best street food. Growing up in such a rich food environment, allowed me to eat, learn and experiment withfood, adding to my passion.

USA is my second home and here too I have been extremely lucky to have found a similar environment. The Bay Area represents a similar melting pot of various ethnic communities with palates to match this diversity. Having treated myself with a variety of food choices here, both Indian as well as other world cuisines, has led me to add to my learning experiences.

My home based catering venture - Flavors, represents a unique amalgamation of all my experiences and I am proud to offer them to my clients. I strive to provide you with exclusive, quality Indian food. Right from mixing the spices, to preparing the base and final cooking is hand made, using the most authentic and best quality ingredients.

I welcome you to experience a world of flavors, brought to you in a unique combination of exclusive, quality dishes.

Thank you very much,

Manasi Dharne
Head Chef & Co-Proprietor
