Dear Guest,

Welcome to the Flavors website. It would be our pleasure to cater food for your next event.

Ever since we can remember, our families and we have been passionate about food. This passion has led us to learn, cook, host and share a varied set of traditional as well as contemporary Indian dishes through the years. Our learning’s have been nurtured through a mix of inquisitiveness as well as generations of knowledge as passed on to us by our lineage. The culmination of these experiences has been our own home-based catering venture, named “Flavors”. The name signifies the presence of varied tastes within the Indian cuisine; We strive to bring all those unique tastes within a combination of exclusive Indian dishes, as offered on our menu.

We have been serving patrons like you for over a year now out of the Bay Area in California, USA. We hope that you shall consider our services for your next party or get together. We guarantee one of a kind food experience and welcome you to taste the difference!
