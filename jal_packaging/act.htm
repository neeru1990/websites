Our Main Speciality

Latex Lining
The crimp-end is lined with latex, so as to prevent leakage. This is an important aspect in case of water and oil-based compounds, since any leakage would destroy the entire pack.

Shoulder Coating
It gives the aluminum tube, the appearance and appeal of laminated tubes

Sealed Nozzle
A sure protection against leakage from the nozzle area, it is widely applied in products such as paints, art material, ointments, etc.

Elongated Nozzle
It has been a welcome change for the eye ointment tubes. Special care is taken to see that no metal particles gain entry into the tube. Also, a thorough cleaning of the tubes has been devised by us.

Plastic Nozzle
A special plastic nozzle. It is a two-pronged safeguard, in that the tube can be totally free of metal particles, and such nozzle can be used for products which are highly reactive and where the lacquer cannot have access to the nozzle area.

Wadless Caps
Previously caps equipped with wads were potential hazard o the contents of the tube, being subject to accumulating dirt and unhygienic elements and being in direct contact with the tube contents.
