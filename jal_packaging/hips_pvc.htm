HIPS/PVC Trays

An Assurance of Safety

A simple system consisting of precisely formed undulation of HIPS/PVC bound to a moulded strip, this international packaging is both convenient and protective.

Available as trays and packs, are firm enough to maintain a secure grip. Horizontal stress and vertical pressure can both be withstood ensuring against breakage and damage. Each unit is complete in itself and in addition is safe and durable in use. Lightweight and recyclable, this packaging is extremely easy to transport, distribute and handle.

A Range Of Uses

Manufactured in different sizes and types, the Rondo packaging is available in the form of HIP & PVC Trays. Plastic trays are ideal for a variety of products that need to be handled with care, such as ampoules, vials, ball pens, cosmetics, food items, etc.
