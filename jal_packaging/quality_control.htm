Manufacturing Practices
  • High standards of hygiene and cleanliness maintained at all times and everywhere.
  • Regular health and hygiene audits.
  • Clean floor maintenance.
  • Regular fumigation and pest control.
  • Complete well-being of staffers.

  • Incoming Materials
    -     All in coming materials are duly inspected
    - All RM & PM predesignated storing area
    - Materials are issued after OC approval on FIFO basis

  • In Process Testing
    This is carried at regular interval by QA personnel and record of the same time is duly registered under following category
    - On line QC report on Extrusion
    - On line QC report on printing
    - On line process control

  • Finished Product
    On completion of production run, complete consignment is subjected to predispatch SQC. and only after approval of final QC report materials are dispatched.
