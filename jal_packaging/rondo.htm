Paper Rondo Trays

An Assurance of Safety

A simple inexpensive system consisting of precisely formed undulations of card-board bonded to a flat backing strip, this international Rondo packaging is both convenient and protective. Available as trays and packs, the air cushioned cartons are resilient enough to yield and firm enough to maintain a secure grip. Horizontal stress and vertical pressure can both be withstood ensuring against breakage and damage. Each unit is complete in itself and additional cushioning in the form of an outer carton is not required. Lightweight and recyclable, this ecofriendly packaging is extremely easy to transport, distribute and handle.

A Range Of Uses

Manufactured in different sizes and types, the Rondo packaging is available in the form of trays and box packs. Trays are ideal for a variety of products that need to be handled with care, such as ampoules, vials, ball pens, cosmetics, electrical fuses, etc. Packs find use in bulk packing for hospitals and export.
