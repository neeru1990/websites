It all started in 1904, when two aspiring brothers landed up in Mumbai, in search of success in the city of dreams. It was this ambition for success, which led to the foundation of MUKTAGIRI PAINTERS, now one of the oldest advertising firms in Mumbai. The firm, which was the brainchild of the younger of the two brothers, Muktagiri Pangaonkar, was established on 16th Jan. 1905.

Success was not easy to come by in those days of heavy taxes, but as the years passed by, the firm grew and it now dealt with the manufacturing and leasing of drama curtains, which were quite a success in those days of theatre.

But, with the theatre industry starting to decline, it was time to explore unknown areas. Thus began the manufacturing of large size hoardings, banners and wooden letters plated with gold. Surprisingly enough, Muktagiri Painters was the only firm, which manufactured and still manufactures these type of letters.

Later on, both of Muktagiri’s sons, Prabhakar and Rajkumar joined the business and helped in obtaining clients like the Union Bank Of India, The Allahabad bank, etc. Muktagiri Painters was now a truly well established and well-settled firm.

The firm is now handled by the grandsons of Muktagiri viz. Ajitkumar and Prashant Pangaonkar. It now has multinational companies like Tata, BPL, Thomas Cook and many others, (a glimpse of whom you may catch on the clients page) as it’s clients.

We shall soon be celebrating a 100 years of success !
