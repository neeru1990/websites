The Power of Gospel ministries Benevolence Program was established based on the word of God, through faith in Jesus Christ our Lord. Our purpose is to meet essential humanitarian and emergency needs in the United States and in India with compassion and love of Christ either directly or in partnership with other organization with like faith, so to do the desire and will of our Lord Jesus Christ.

By the mercies and grace of God, this ministry was able to perform the following activities to fulfill the purpose of God in Christ. Each of the activities were planned and executed to meet urgent basic needs of our fellow men and bring glory and honor to God our Father.

Katrina Relief at Louisiana

God want us to be a good neighbor, a good neighbor is the one who would care to pause for a moment when his neighbor is in trouble. God's love for man kind that was shown in the cross of Christ never fails, but in the compassion of God, moves to help a neighbor in need and especially at the day of trouble.
Oct. 1, 2005 When hurricane hit New Orleans, we joined hands with Pastor John Benjamin Peters with Faith Living Word Fellowship at Natchitoches, Louisiana to help them build showers and toilet facilities to setup a shelter that operated for few months to help evacuees from New Orleans. We provided financial help to rebuild the life of one family trying to reestablish after they had lost all they had in Katrina.

Jan. 1, 2005

 When Tsunami waves of destruction hit South East Asian countries, we joined with ministries in India and Sri Lanka to extend a hand of support. By the grace of God, we have provided assistance for 12 families who lost all they had in the destruction. We provided a coconut fiber weaving machine and raw materials to a family who had lost their equipment and raw materials during the Tsunami. We provided financial assistance to replace and replenish a poultry farm for a widow who had lost her husband, three children and the family poultry farm. The stories are heart-breaking.
