AVT, makers of RightFAX, is pleased to announce a special, limited-time offer for Lotus Fax for Domino customers. As the worldwide market leader in enterprise fax solutions, RightFAX products are acclaimed for their reliability, flexibility, scalability and ease of use and installation. As a Lotus preferred fax offering, RightFAX offers Fax for Domino customers a quick, easy and seamless migration to the RightFAX Gateway for Lotus Notes.

The RightFAX Gateway for Lotus Notes offers a robust architecture that lets Notes users send and receive faxes directly from their familiar Notes client. For users, sending and receiving a fax is as simple as sending an e-mail. For Notes administrators, RightFAX is easy to install and manage. The RightFAX gateway architecture allows for the RightFAX and Domino servers to operate independently. This means that RightFAX faxing capabilities are not impacted by scheduled or unscheduled downtime that may interrupt the Domino server.
