TR1034™ Features & Benefits

  • V.34 33.6Kbps fax transmission rates for increased performance and lower cost of ownership.
  • High fax port density per server to minimize space requirements.
  • Onboard T1/E1 telephony interfaces for simpler installation and a small footprint.
  • Onboard image conversion to off-load processing from the host.
  • Adaptive call progress to connect with a wide variety of fax machines.
  • MH, MR, MMR fax compression to reduce transmission time.
  • Error Correction Mode (ECM) to provide reliable and fast transmissions.
  • Super Fine Fax Resolution (406 x 391 dots per inch) for increased document readability and clearer images.
