TR114 Series

PCI and ISA Intelligent Fax and Voice Boards

The TR114™ is acknowledged as the leading intelligent fax board by market analysts. Continually refined since its introduction in 1994, the TR114 has achieved unparalleled levels of system reliability, compatibility, fax machine connectivity, and average overall transmission speeds. The TR114 Series empowers messaging software applications to address a wide range of customer environments. For the enterprise user today there are more than 30 LAN fax software packages for use with the TR114 Series. The TR114 is approved by telecommunications regulatory agencies worldwide in over 30 countries. Densities range from 1 to 16 channels on a single board and up to 96 channels per chassis. International service providers requiring carrier-class performance and entrepreneurial application development companies requiring development flexibility have built their businesses on the TR114 Series platform. Brooktrout’s ongoing commitment to consistent API support ensures applications written for the TR114 will be compatible with TRxStream™ Series boards in the future.

HARDWARE
  • Fax/Speech Channels/Board: 1, 2, 4, 8, 12, 16
  • Processors: NS32FXI 64 Integrated CISC and DSP @ 25MHz
  • Host System Interface: ISA
    – Interrupt: 3–7, 9–12, 14, 15
    – I/O addresses: 12/20/36
    – DMA channel: 8-bit, 0–3; 16-bft or bit, 5–7 software-selectable
  • Host System Interface: PCI
    – Interrupt: Assigned by Host
    – I/O addresses: Assigned by Host
    – DMA channel: none
  • Telephone Interfaces: Loop Start, DID,
    European ISDN BRI, T1
  • Telephony Bus Interface: MVIP, PEB
  • Programmable Transmit Levels
  • Dialing and Call Progress: On-board DTMF generation and detection
  • Call Progress
    – Adaptive (S.I.T. detection included), Dialing, Pulse and DTMF
    – DID and DNIS Digit Capture
  • 8 Downloadable Fonts
  • Self Diagnostics
  • Speech Features: 24 and 32kbps ADPCM, 64kbps PCM
  • Approvals and Certifications
    – UL: 1459
    – FCC: Pan 15, Part 68
    – DCC: CS03
