Summary

The HP 9100C Digital Sender--the speed of a fax and the quality of an original for the cost of an e-mail.

Information is the lifeblood of any enterprise. The more quickly, easily and inexpensively a business can share it, the greater their competitive advantage. Which explains why electronic transmission has become the preferred method of exchange.

That's why HP is introducing the HP 9100C Digital Sender, an innovative new workgroup communication device that converts paper documents into digital information so they can be sent via e-mail. But that's not all. The HP 9100C Digital Sender can also send information to a network fax solution, HP JetSend-enabled device, network PC or network printer

Features

Send just about anything. Anywhere. Anytime.

With the HP 9100C Digital Sender, you can send almost any paper-based information whenever and wherever you want. Brochures, contracts, bills of lading or medical images are compressed and converted to PDF (or TIFF) files that can be sent to several different people-in different places-at once.

Once the information is received, it can be stored, printed, edited or reformatted. Try that with a stand-alone fax. And because it can be delivered right to the recipient's desk, it's ideal for sensitive or confidential information.

As easy as e-mail because it is e-mail

The HP 9100C Digital Sender can be installed in about five minutes with no modifications to your IT infrastructure and without extensive user training. Just plug it in and anybody who knows how to send an e-mail can start using the Digital Sender right away.

All you do is 1. place your document in the automatic feeder, 2. type in or select a destination from an address list, and 3. Press the green "send" button. That's all there is to it.

Just like that, your hardcopy documents are converted and compressed into small digital files, complete with high-quality black and white or full color images, and sent to anyone with an e-mail address.

As fast as an e-mail

If you've ever sent documents abroad via overnight mail, you know it can take days for your shipment to reach its destination.

Not with the HP 9100C Digital Sender. Whatever you send travels at the speed of e-mail. And HP Smart Compression technology shrinks complex color documents for even faster transmission. When it has to be there now, instead of later, it has to be the HP 9100C Digital Sender.

The quality of an original

These days, many documents combine text and color graphics, which usually means huge files that clog networks. But HP has solved the problem with HP Smart Compression technology. It converts documents into small, compressed PDF files that are automatically optimized in all areas-text, photos and line art. The result is near-original quality with crisp, clean text and clear black and white or full color images. Whether you're sending text or photos, you'll get high quality files that have a minimal impact on valuable network bandwidth.

Less expensive than overnight mail or fax

Since the HP 9100C Digital Sender distributes information via your existing e-mail network, it costs much less than courier services. It's even less expensive than the long distance charges you pay for faxes.

And you'll save more than money. Because sending documents with the HP 9100C Digital Sender is so easy, you'll save administrative time too. You can send information to multiple recipients at the same time, and there are no courier labels to fill out or pickup deadlines to meet. And desktop delivery means you'll never waste time tracking down someone who's on the road.

Best of all, the HP 9100C Digital Sender features the quality, reliability and service you expect from HP.

To send paper documents with the speed of a fax and the quality of an original for the cost of an e-mail, the choice is clear: the HP 9100C Digital Sender.
