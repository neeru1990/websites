FAX BOARDS
RightFax fax server software is supported by Brooktrout and Intel Dialogic intelligent fax boards. Generally, at least one fax board must be installed in the computer where the fax server software is running.

Captaris makes intelligent fax board selection and purchase easy with complete sales and support—no need to undertake the extra step of going to the board manufacturer. The RightFax board replacement plan, complimentary for the first year, offers expedited replacement to protect against potential board defects.

Intelligent fax boards contain on-board microprocessors that support advanced fax compression methods, convert documents to fax format and can manage fax phone calls via on-board processing power.  Fax modems, alternatively, rely exclusively on the power of PC microprocessors, which do not allow for advanced fax compression.  Fax modems therefore cannot handle the high throughput speeds, image handling, and automated processing provided by RightFax.

RightFax uses Brooktrout and Intel Dialogic boards exclusively because of their compatibility, throughput speeds, call progress, computer loading, image handling, automated processing of received faxes, programmability, ability for patching and upgrading, system integration readiness, overseas usage approvals, and vendor support.
