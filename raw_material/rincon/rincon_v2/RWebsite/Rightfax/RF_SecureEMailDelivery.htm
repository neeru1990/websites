RIGHTFAX SECURE E-MAIL DELIVERY
AN OVERVIEW

In today’s business environment, having the flexibility to deliver important business communications securely and reliably either by fax or email, as well as having confirmation that important communications reached the intended recipient, is vital to conducting business electronically.

Now, RightFax with the optional SecureDocs module, brings together the best of fax and email, offering companies a centralized, reliable, easy-to-use and manage e-document delivery solution that is reliable and secure.

SecureDocs allows for email to be sent using digital encryption technology to secure a document’s transmission from sender to receiver, and to protect against tampering. SecureDocs also provides an enhanced notifications feature utilizing a Certified Delivery process.

By adding RightFax SecureDocs module to a RightFax Business or Enterprise Server, you gain the ability to send documents securely, with the assurance that its intended recipient safely received the document.

HOW IT WORKS

With SecureDocs, users can send documents securely from their Windows desktop, from the RightFax FaxUtil client, or from their accounting, mainframe, ERP or host system using the RightFax InternetLink Module.  The document is digitally encrypted and password protected so that documents can be delivered electronically without worry of tampering.  In addition, SecureDocs can also provide certified delivery, which ensures notification of its receipt.  Using certified delivery, the documents are delivered as a message to the recipient’s mailbox, and posted on a Web site.  The recipient can retrieve the password protected document on the Web site using a standard Web browser.  A confirmation with the date and time the document was opened is then sent back to the sender, completing the certified delivery cycle.

Easily send confirmed, secure email:Create and send secure, confirmed email messages with document attachments from desktop applications, RightFax FaxUtil, or your host-based application like CRM, or ERP systems. This provides a low-cost, secure electronic delivery option for a company’s most important business communications.

Recipients receive confidential documents: Recipients of secure documents can retrieve them easily and access the encrypted attachment using a password.

Verification of delivery: To authenticate that the document reached its intended recipient, an email notification is sent back to the sender with a time and date stamp for easy tracking.

Confirmed mission-critical document delivery via email: Creates an email notification telling the document’s originator that the document was successfully delivered and opened.

Secure automated document delivery: Further leverage the e-process automation capabilities of RightFax Universal Information Exchange systems with the inclusion of secure email delivery of mission-critical communications.

Easy administration and auditing: Easily track and monitor the delivery status of your mission-critical documents by taking advantage of confirmation notification features. This enables organizations to keep track of when important documents were delivered and read.

Support for high-resolution, color documents: SecureDocs supports rich PDF format to give you the alternative to deliver higher resolution or color documents.

FEATURES

  • Provides digital document encryption to ensure confidential and secure delivery via email
  • Formats documents as HTML so that it can be viewed from standard browsers
  • Confirmation of delivery occurs when the documents are opened
  • Leverages your existing RightFax Business Server or Enterprise Server (version 8.0 or higher)
  • Provides notification of delivery using standard RightFax notification methods
  • Maintains complete audit trail through RightFax server and writes a history record for each time an individual page is accessed
  • Enables monitoring of audit trail via FaxUtil for ease of administration       
  • Timestamps document each time it is accessed to track authenticity
  • Ability to set various security parameters on outbound documents
  • Customize retrieval Web pages with company logos, tag lines and graphics 

Requirements

  • RightFax Business or Enterprise Server (version 8.0 or higher)
  • SecureDocs Module
  • Internet Information Service (IIS)
  • RightFax InternetLink and Integration Modules (for Universal Information Exchange functionality)
