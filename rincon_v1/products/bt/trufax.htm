TruFax 100 and 200

Available in PCI and ISA Form Factors with one and two channel configurations.

Proven Reliability

Brooktrout fax boards are renowned for reliable 24 hour, day in/day out performance. The TruFax ® 100 and 200 multichannel fax boards are no exception. TruFax is available in PCI and ISA form factors and in 1 and 2 channel configurations.

The TruFax 100/200 features Brooktrout’s field proven T.30 fax protocol technology and adaptive call progress to provide the best connectivity ratios available. When compared to off-the-shelf data modems, this high performance dramatically reduces the need for costly human intervention and time-consuming retry of fax transmissions/receptions.

Since all fax sessions are controlled by on-the-board technology, the resulting performance maximizes throughput without draining the host CPU, allowing the system to work efficiently on other important functions, even under peak traffic loads. And the TruFax 100/200’s low power consumption and heat dissipation minimizes overall system cost and maintenance..

High Performance Features

The TruFax fax boards support the following fax features: 14.4kbps transmission; MH compression; "on-the-fly" conversion of ASCII, TIFF and PCX files; DTMF generation and detection; as well as call progress handling.

Entry-Level Platform

The TruFax 100/200 represents a reliable entry point for fax server communications. With one and two channel configurations available, TruFax is ideal for work groups and medium size busi-nesses today, while providing a scalable growth platform for the future. TruFax boards support up to 12 channels in a single server.

Leading Application Support

The TruFax, TR114 ™ and TRxStream ™ products work with the same Brooktrout Fax and Voice API which allows developers to create a variety of applications that scale in cost and functionality across our entire product line. Brooktrout’s Fax and Voice API is a powerful tool for sophisticated application development. High-level functions permit programmers to quickly create applications. Lower level functions permit direct interactions with individual elements of fax and voice calls, such as the T.30 protocol.

Brooktrout fax platforms are supported by all major enhanced and network fax software products, providing maximum flexibility in designing a total solution.
