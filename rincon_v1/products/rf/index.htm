The Captaris RightFax line of products provides easy-to-use, easy-to-manage, enterprise-wide fax offerings that seamlessly integrate with business applications. By combining fax, email and Internet technologies, RightFax provides a one-stop, full service e-document delivery solution that gives companies the freedom and control to tailor a document delivery environment to fit the growing and changing needs of their business. With a range of on-premise and outsourced e-document delivery options, RightFax is uniquely positioned as the single source for all e-document delivery needs.
