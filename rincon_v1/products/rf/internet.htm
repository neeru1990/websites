Captaris RightFax Internet Fax Services

With Internet fax via the RightFax IP Plus Connector, a RightFax Enterprise Server can deliver documents via a secure IP connection over the Captaris MediaLinq network for distribution to their final destinations.

The IP Plus Connector provides redundancy and backup in case of unexpected loss of phone service, fax board failure, or rerouting of traffic during server maintenance. It can also increase your capacity on demand to handle uncommonly high fax traffic volume.

Additionally, the RightFax IP Plus Connector lets you send large fax broadcast jobs to the RightFax IP Plus Service for immediate or simultaneous delivery to multiple destinations worldwide. Providing virtually unlimited fax port capacity, the connector is transparent to the desktop user.

For more information, visit the Captaris RightFax IP Plus Web site.
