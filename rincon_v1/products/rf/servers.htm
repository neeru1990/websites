Captaris RightFax Network & Enterprise Fax

Captaris RightFax network and enterprise fax solutions are scalable and server-based to best meet your needs. Select the best RightFax server or suite for your needs based on your company size:

  • Large corporate enterprises
  • Mid-size companies and departments
  • Small companies and workgroups

Large corporate enterprises can choose from these fax servers:

RightFax Enterprise Server
Ideal for national and global businesses that require mission-critical reliability to handle heavy network traffic and optimized document delivery across all systems and locations. Combine the power of multiple RightFax servers on your LAN, WAN, Internet or intranet. Review features.

RightFax Enterprise Suite
A bundle of the RightFax Enterprise Server with eight RightFax modules for full functionality at an affordable price.

RightFax Satellite Server
Allows a branch office to enjoy the high-performance capabilities of an Enterprise system at an affordable price. For branch offices with up to 15 users, the RightFax Satellite Server is a specially licensed fax server and email gateway (choose from the RightFax Gateway for Microsoft Exchange or the Gateway for Lotus Notes) that works with a RightFax Enterprise server.

RightFax Add-on Modules and Connectors
RightFax modules let you expand your RightFax system to add functionality that meets your company's needs and existing business applications. Review add-on modules and connectors.
