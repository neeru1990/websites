IP Plus Integration FAQ

Q. Can I set up multiple IP Plus accounts for different departments?
A. Yes. You can configure as many separate IP Plus accounts in Enterprise Fax Manager as you want. Each dialing rule lets you specify which IP Plus account to use, and dialing rules can be restricted to only certain RightFax users or groups.

Q. Will I be notified when my account is getting low?
A. Yes. You will be notified via email when your account balance falls to 20% remaining and again when it is depleted.

Q. What is the CSID used for faxes I send via IP Plus? Is this configurable?
A. All faxes sent via the IP Plus service use the CSID "RightFAX v7.0 IP Plus." In v7.0, this CSID cannot be modified.

Q. Is there a billing multiplier for high resolution faxes?
A. Yes. To account for the extra transmission time required, each high resolution fax page is charged as two fax pages.

Q. What are the billing multipliers for international fax transmission?
A. Faxes sent to destinations outside the continental United States (including to Alaska and Hawaii) may be charged a per-page multiplier.
