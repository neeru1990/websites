Xerox Integration FAQ

Q. How does RightFax work with the Xerox Document Centre?
A. Using RightFax with the Document Centre, users have both easy walk-up access and a single, central point of control for electronic and hard copy faxing. Working in conjunction with a RightFax server, Document Centre users no longer need to maintain stand-alone fax machines or dedicated phone lines or modems for individual workstations. Instead, they can easily send and receive faxes directly from the Document Centre on their network with RightFax and take advantage of cost-saving features such as Intelligent Least-Cost Routing™, as well as a list of other capabilities such as inbound routing, advanced notifications, and easy fax management.

Q. How does outbound faxing work?
A. Xerox hardware/software captures paper documents and integrates them electronically into the RightFax server. The user programs the fax job using the touch screen user interface, presses the green button and scans in a document to be faxed. The Document Centre converts the document into a TIFF file and attaches the fax instructions. The TIFF file is sent to the RightFax server. RightFax software converts the TIFF to CCITT GIV and transmits the document. A transmission report can be sent from the RightFax server to the Document Centre and printed.

Q. How does inbound faxing work?
A. An incoming fax arrives at the RightFax server where it can be sent to any Document Centre on the network and received in the normal queue as an incoming print job where it can be printed or routed electronically for distribution and retrieval.

ABOUT THE RIGHTFAX PRODUCT LINE

Captaris RightFax products provides a total approach to enterprise communications with a complete range of network, production, and Internet delivery options that integrate with desktop, email, CRM, ERP, document management, and other business applications:

  • RightFax network-based solutions provide the desktop and email gateways needed to enable any desktop user to send, receive, and manage documents. RightFax network solutions provide a range of server lines and add-on modules to meet the document delivery needs of organizations of any size.
  • RightFax production systems enable electronic delivery via fax, email, or the Internet of business-critical documents such as purchase orders, invoices, and statements automatically and unattended from legacy, host, or ERP applications.
