RightFax

SAP Integration

BUSINESS INTEGRATION

The entry-level universal information exchange solution for organizations with low volume transactions that want to automate document delivery of business-critical documents from a single SAP application.

Integrates with: SAP R/3 3.1g and later. Limited to one SAP input.

RightFax requirements: Requires either the RightFax Enterprise or Business server. Additional RightFax modules can be added for Internet delivery. Boards, channel licenses, support and services sold separately. See server requirements for more specific information.

Features:

  • Integrates with one SAP application via SAPconnect, SAPscript or SAP Smart Forms
  • Enables one document form for routine automated distribution
  • Provides low volume throughput- approximately 300 pages per day
  • Fax and print delivery only. Email and Internet delivery sold separately.
  • Supports two integration channel licenses (expandable to 30 or 1,024 total channels depending on server option) for integration traffic only.
  • Unlimited user licenses.
