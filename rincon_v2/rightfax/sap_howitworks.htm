RightFax

SAP Integration

HOW IT WORKS

RightFax Four Phase Workflow Process: RightFax utilizes a Four Phase Workflow Process that captures information from SAP, renders it into an electronic image, distributes the information via fax, email or the Internet and creates customized reporting.


Phase 1: Data Recognition
RightFax UIX receives input, in a variety of formats, from SAP.


Phase 2: Information Assembly
Using one of the various information assembly methods available, information from SAP is rendered into an electronic image.


Phase 3: Information Distribution
Information is delivered to the recipient. A variety of methods can be used to deliver documents, depending on the RightFax UIX package, including fax, email and delivery over the Internet.


Phase 4: Reporting
Notifications of delivery for reporting and audit trail are sent to the host or end user. Different levels of notification are available depending on the RightFax UIX package.
