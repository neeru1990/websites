Zetafax

Benefits

Zetafax offers your company significant time savings over conventional fax methods.

A rich range of features for both users and administrators contribute to fast, easy and cost effective faxing either in the office or on the road. With the SMS add-on feature, and the ability to fax from virtually any application, Zetafax brings together all your fax, email and text communications in one place.

With the ability to make faxing as quick and straightforward as printing a document or sending an email, Zetafax is the obvious way to improve your company's communications efficiency.

Fax anywhere
  • Fax wherever you are
    Zetafax extends your fax communications so employees don't have to be sitting at their desks. They can be out on the road visiting clients, partners and suppliers.

  • Faxing from a laptop
    Zetafax lets you send and receive faxes anywhere. Zetafax detects if your laptop is connected to the fax server. If not, the locally installed version of the fax server starts up and operates using the laptop's fax modem.

  • All your fax tools - as if you were in the office
    Using Zetafax on your laptop is just the same as using it at your desk. When you're away from the office, fax coversheets, letterheads and archives go with you.

  • Check fax and email at the same time
    Zetafax can forward any fax sent to your own personal direct dial fax number to an email account. So when you pick up your email you can pick up your faxes too.
Fax as easy as email

Zetafax is supplied with an email gateway so all your faxing can be performed within your email client user interface.

  • Integrated fax and email
    Using Zetafax in your email client is the easiest way to fax. During set up, Zetafax installs a new button in your email client which users click on whenever they want to send a new fax. Likewise, when a new fax arrives, it appears in the Inbox just like an email. Zetafax supports the following email systems:
    • Microsoft Exchange
    • Lotus Domino
    • SMTP

  • Sending faxes from your email client
    In your email client, faxes can be written just like email, using a coversheet if desired, and can be previewed before sending. Documents such as Word files and spreadsheets can be attached and are rendered into faxable TIFF format by Zetafax.

  • Reading faxes just like email
    Receiving faxes via the email gateway is just as straightforward. If the Zetafax direct dial inward (DDI) routing option is enabled, faxes are routed straight to your email Inbox. The Zetafax viewer allows faxes to be sized, rotated and amended before forwarding them on.

  • Enhance your marketing with fax shots just like email
    Setting up personalised fax broadcasts is quick and easy using Zetafax and merging the contacts from your customer database or Outlook contacts. Zetafax notifies you of any transmissions that fail, with the option of resubmitting them to the fax server and printing out failed faxes so you can send them by mail.
Fax faster

Zetafax saves the physical effort of walking to the fax machine to pick up or send a fax, and also saves receptionists and secretaries the trouble of distributing from a central point.

Simply select the 'Send' button on your PC, and when the Zetafax addressing box appears, select the recipient's details from your Microsoft Outlook address book and send the fax.

Sending a fax via a conventional fax machine can take up to five minutes. Typing and printing the document to be faxed ... walking to the printer to collect the fax … walking to the fax machine ... dialling the fax number ... waiting for the fax to transmit and obtaining your confirmation ... walking to the filing cabinet ... filing the fax ... returning to your desk.

With Zetafax, faxing becomes much faster.

Fax for less

Zetafax starts paying for itself from the minute it is installed, and will save you more as you add additional users. The return on your investment in Zetafax will easily justify your initial outlay. But it is not just users who benefit. Before a single fax has been sent with Zetafax, the web client can save time on installation, as it enables users across the organization without the need for dedicated client installations.

  • Faxes direct to every desktop
    Distributing faxes manually takes time, which in turn costs money. With Zetafax, faxes are delivered automatically to your employees' PCs at their desktop - an altogether more efficient, cost effective way of working.

  • Return on investment
    Labor savings alone quickly justify the initial outlay on Zetafax. Sending a fax via a fax machine can take up to five minutes, but using Zetafax takes just one minute - an 80% time saving.

  • Consolidate your telephone lines
    The Zetafax server supports multiple telephone lines. Which means you can reduce the cost of line rental by centralizing your telephone lines and fax hardware on a single PC.

  • Lower stationery and consumables costs
    Since Zetafax sends and delivers directly from and to desktop PCs, your company's outlay on fax toner and thermal fax paper is significantly reduced.

  • Simple administration controls
    Your administrator can determine who can send faxes, where and when. For instance, users can be restricted from sending international faxes and usage limits can be set to restrict faxing only to off-peak times.

  • Flexibility and scalability
    For sites with low fax usage, Zetafax runs on an existing server with a fax modem. As your fax usage grows, and you add more users, Zetafax supports intelligent fax boards and multiple fax lines - reducing transmission times and call costs.

  • Low cost faxing for all your offices
    If your company has multiple, distributed sites, the Zetafax Least Cost Routing module lets you send faxes quickly and cheaply using the Internet. International faxes are sent at local call rates, providing substantial cost savings.
